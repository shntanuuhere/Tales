Tales - Wallpapers & Podcasts

Tales is a beautiful app that combines stunning wallpapers with engaging podcasts, all in one elegant package.

Save your favorite wallpapers for easy access later. Browse wallpapers by category to find exactly what you're looking for.

Enjoy Tales in light or dark mode, whichever suits your preference.

Downloaded wallpapers and podcasts are available offline, so you can enjoy them anywhere.

Our clean, intuitive interface makes it easy to navigate between wallpapers and podcasts.
